Tales From Soda Island

"Tales from Soda Island" will send you on an immersive journey through the vibrant and complex musical ecosystem of Soda Island. You'll discover strange creatures, ancient rituals and cosmic symphonies working together in harmony to produce a story unlike anything you've ever seen before!

This VR animated series is an anthology of events that unfold on Soda Island. Each story is unique and follows diverse themes, with a central narrative thread woven throughout the series. The series was entirely created in VR using Quill by Smoothstep. Envision an entire production spanning 3 years being created entirely in VR by each artist and animator. The series and all of its content is over 2 hours long and has over millions of views on the MetaQuest Platform.

I encourage you to go and watch the series for yourself on MetaQuest because it is nothing like you have ever seen, the 2D format does not do it justice. Here is a link to a playlist for each Chapter, The Behind the Scenes, and our Profile.

Achievement

This is one of the greatest projects I have had the honor of being a part of. The Soda Island world is a marvelous experience. I call it a “virtual hug”. I promise you, it will improve your wellbeing after you immerse yourself in the playful and thrilling world that is soda island. We had an exceptional team working on this project, under the creative leadership of Simone Fougnier. Thanks to Meta, who recognized our passion utilizing Quill to create content for their platform. We seized the opportunity to make some of the most innovative and creative additions to the expanding world of VR. The series has been recongized by The Venice Film Festival and Siggraph.

Ownership

At Studio Syro, I led complete project oversight, fostering collaboration across all production levels, from executive producers, director, artists, animators, audio directors, and sound designers. My role involved guiding initial pitches, and collaborating with the director through every stage of the production. I managed the early ideation phases, scriptwriting, storyboarding, development of animatics, rough cuts, leading up to the alpha and final release stages. Leveraging my creative and technical background, I contributed to storytelling and tackled challenges for 3D implementation. Overseeing task management, managing deadlines, and handing all forms communication with key stakeholders for our production were key components that collectively defined my multifaceted role at Studio Syro. Building and hiring the right team was crucial, as was overseeing budgeting, payroll, and legal review processes. I was the primary individual for spatial audio work for each experience and IT support throughout production. I also spent R&D time for technical improvements to our pipeline and software. Simultaneously, I handled various studio obligations related to running a business.
